## Don't wear about me!

Android Wear project made during the TaxiHack http://taxihack.it, the first hackathon in the world of taxi.

Is a project about multi card notifications to the smartwatch during a taxi ride, that automate various actions on your smartphone such as: display the map, send an email or call a phone number.
